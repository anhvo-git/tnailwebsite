Welcome to T Nail Spa in Glentmont Center

Our team of highly skilled technicians is dedicated to enhancing your natural beauty with a range of exceptional services. Delight in opulent manicures and pedicures that leave your hands and feet pampered, polished, and feeling extraordinary. Unleash your personal style with breathtaking nail art and relish in the enduring elegance of our gel nail treatments that are trendy and relevant. We refresh our brands and colors seasonally so that you always stay in fashion. Mesmerize with captivating eyelash extensions, enhancing the allure of your eyes. For flawless skin, experience the artistry of our professional waxing services, ensuring a silky-smooth finish. At our nail salon, we believe in the perfect fusion of sophistication, expertise, and rejuvenation. Safety and comfort are paramount to us, and we uphold the highest industry standards in cleanliness. Our commitment to your well-being includes thorough sanitation and disinfection of all reusable nail implements adhering to state regulations using an autoclave when appropriate. All one- time use items are promptly disposed of for your peace of mind. Book an appointment with us today and allow us to pamper you with unrivaled care and indulgence.
